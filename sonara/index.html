The Origin

Built in Namibia.
For everywhere else.

Wolfgang was deep in Namibia — surrounded by the hypnotic pulse of Southern African rhythms. The music was incredible. But his Spotify knew nothing about it.

"The music there is incredible and I wanted it in my life without losing myself."

He didn't want to start over with a blank slate. He wanted his taste — his energy, his moods, his musical identity — blended with the sounds of wherever he was in the world.

That frustration became Sonara. A bridge between who you are musically and the culture you're stepping into.
