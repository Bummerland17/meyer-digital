## Key Observations

- **Jackson Hole market is especially strong**: High-end homes, affluent clients, high job values. Even one new client from a website pays for the $599 site 5–20x over. Great pitch angle.
- **Yahoo/Gmail email addresses** are a reliable signal of businesses that haven't updated anything digital since 2010–2015.
- **HTTP-only sites** (no HTTPS) are actively penalized by Google and show security warnings — easy conversation starter.
- **Star Valley** has several businesses with zero web presence, relying on a local community directory (starvalleywy.com) — a segment with virtually no digital competition.
- **Cheyenne** has multiple electricians with weak or outdated sites. Solid warm-lead pool.
