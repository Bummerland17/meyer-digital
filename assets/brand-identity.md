# BRAND 1: VELDT

> *Parent company. Personal brand. The open ground from which everything grows.*

---

## 1. Logo Concept

**Shape:** A single horizontal line — thin, clean, unbroken — stretching edge to edge. Above it, a minimal landscape suggestion: flat land meeting infinite sky. Could be interpreted as a horizon, a growth chart, or a runway.

**Feel:** Understated authority. Not a startup logo — a house logo. Something that could appear on a letterhead, a building, or a passport cover without looking out of place.

**Colors:** Deep charcoal text on white, or white reversed on near-black. A thin gold or warm amber horizon line as the sole accent — evoking African sunset, warmth, and permanence.

**Symbol option:** The letter V as two converging lines that form a landscape silhouette — land narrowing into a distant point, conveying both direction and openness.

---

## 2. Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Primary | Deep Earth | `#1a1208` |
| Secondary | Savanna Stone | `#c4a882` |
| Accent | Horizon Gold | `#d4a853` |
| Background | Bone White | `#f7f4ef` |
| Text Dark | Midnight Soil | `#0f0d0a` |

**Feel:** Warm, grounded, timeless. Not corporate navy. Not tech purple. This is earth.

---

## 3. Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display / Headings | **Cormorant Garamond** | Light 300, Semibold 600 |
| Body | **Inter** | Regular 400, Medium 500 |
| Accent / Labels | **Space Grotesk** | Medium 500 |

> Cormorant brings old-world gravitas. Inter keeps it readable. Space Grotesk bridges to the tech side (shared with Wolfpack AI).

---

## 4. Brand Voice

**Three adjectives:** Expansive · Assured · Deliberate

**Never says:**
- "Disrupting the industry" — *Veldt builds, it doesn't disrupt*
- "Passionate about innovation" — *generic filler that means nothing*

**Says instead:**
- "Built from the ground up."
- "We move when it matters."
- "From one territory to the next."

---

## 5. Elevator Pitch

> Veldt is Wolfgang Meyer's holding company — a platform for ventures built from the open ground of Namibia outward, across industries and borders.

---

## 6. The Story (About Page — 2 Paragraphs)

**Paragraph 1:**
> The veldt is the open grassland of southern Africa — flat, honest, and without pretense. You can see everything coming from miles away. That's the philosophy behind this company: build in the open, move with intention, and don't mistake noise for progress. Veldt was founded by Wolfgang Meyer as a holding ground for the ventures, tools, and ideas worth building — not for any single industry, but for the long game.

**Paragraph 2:**
> Wolfgang grew up in Namibia, where the horizon isn't a boundary — it's an invitation. That instinct — to look at open space and see possibility rather than emptiness — is the founding principle of everything under the Veldt umbrella. Each company, product, and project here starts from the same place: a clear idea, the patience to build it right, and the ambition to take it further than anyone expected.

---

## 7. Tagline Decision

| Tagline | Verdict |
|---------|---------|
| "Built from the open ground up" | ✅ **Primary** — roots the brand geographically and philosophically |
| "Wide open. Moving fast." | ✅ **Secondary / campaign** — good for social, motion content |
| "From the veldt to the world." | ✅ **Storytelling** — use in About page, long-form content |

**Recommended primary:** *"Built from the open ground up."*

# BRAND 2: WOLFPACK AI

> *The revenue engine. A pack of AI agents deployed to hunt for your business.*

---

## 1. Logo Concept

**Shape:** A stylized wolf head — not cartoon, not mascot. Geometric. Sharp angles, minimal detail. Think: the wolf reduced to its essential geometry — jawline, ear, eye. One eye rendered in electric blue, glowing.

**Feel:** Predatory precision. Dark background, white or light grey wolf form, single electric blue accent on the eye or collar. This is a weapon logo — it should look like it means business.

**Alternative:** The letters WP or WΛ (W-Alpha) as a monogram — angular, tight, like a brand burned into steel.

---

## 2. Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Background | Void Black | `#0a0a0a` |
| Primary Text | Arctic White | `#f0f0f0` |
| Accent | Electric Blue | `#0066ff` |
| Secondary Accent | Steel | `#8899aa` |
| Alert / CTA | Hot Blue | `#3385ff` |

**Feel:** A terminal running at 3am. Focused aggression. Cold precision. Nothing warm here — this brand hunts.

---

## 3. Typography

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| All headings | **Space Grotesk** | Bold 700, Semibold 600 |
| Body / UI | **Space Grotesk** | Regular 400, Medium 500 |
| Code / Stats | **JetBrains Mono** | Regular 400 |

> Single-font brand. Space Grotesk handles everything — it's technical but not cold, bold but not shouty. The mono font for numbers gives a live-dashboard feel.

---

## 4. Brand Voice

**Three adjectives:** Relentless · Direct · Tactical

**Never says:**
- "We're excited to announce…" — *Wolfpack doesn't announce, it deploys*
- "Leverage synergies" — *absolutely not*

**Says instead:**
- "Your pack is live."
- "We don't chase leads. We close them."
- "Deployed. Hunting. Reporting back."

---

## 5. Elevator Pitch

> Wolfpack AI deploys a coordinated team of AI sales agents — Scout, Quill, Hawk, and the closers — that research your leads, send personalized outreach, and hand warm prospects directly to you.

---

## 6. The Story (About Page — 2 Paragraphs)

**Paragraph 1:**
> Wolfpack AI was born from a simple observation: most businesses don't have a sales problem — they have a scale problem. The pitch is good. The product is real. But there aren't enough hours in the day to reach every prospect, follow up on every lead, and still run the business. We built Wolfpack to solve exactly that. A coordinated team of AI agents that works around the clock, identifies your best prospects, and puts them on your calendar.

**Paragraph 2:**
> We called it Wolfpack because wolves don't hunt alone, and they don't hunt randomly. They coordinate. They're patient. And when they move, they move together. That's the model: Scout finds the targets, Quill writes the outreach, Hawk tracks the follow-ups, and the closers — Alex, Rex, Kai — handle the handoff to you. You don't manage them. You just close the deals they bring in.

---

## 7. Tagline Decision

| Tagline | Verdict |
|---------|---------|
| "We deploy AI sales teams." | ✅ **Primary** — literal, bold, memorable |
| "Your pack. Always hunting." | ✅ **Secondary** — use in ads, email subject lines |
| "AI agents that close." | ✅ **Ultra-short** — for social bios, metadata |

**Recommended primary:** *"We deploy AI sales teams."*

# BRAND ARCHITECTURE: HOW THEY RELATE

```
VELDT
└── Parent company / personal brand for Wolfgang Meyer
    ├── Philosophy: Build across territories, for the long game
    ├── Audience: Investors, collaborators, press, future hires
    ├── Domain: veldt.co (check if owned) or veldt.build
    │
    └── WOLFPACK AI  ← product venture under Veldt
            ├── Purpose: AI sales pipeline agency / product
            ├── Audience: SMBs, founders, sales teams
            ├── Domain: wolfpackai.io ← AVAILABLE, grab now
            └── Revenue model: Setup fee + monthly retainer
```

**The relationship in one line:**  
*Veldt is the land. Wolfpack AI is what hunts across it.*

**Visual bridge:** Both brands share Space Grotesk as a typography element — it's the thread connecting the grounded parent to the technical product. Veldt adds Cormorant for warmth; Wolfpack AI strips it back to pure function.

**On the Wolfpack AI website:** A small footer line — *"A Veldt company"* — with a link to veldt.co. Subtle. Professional. Signals that there's something bigger behind this product.

## Master Brand Rules

1. **Veldt** never uses electric blue — that belongs to Wolfpack AI
2. **Wolfpack AI** never uses warm earth tones — that's Veldt's territory
3. Both use Space Grotesk → it's the DNA they share
4. Wolfpack AI always says it's "a Veldt company" somewhere — builds trust
5. Veldt's voice is measured and expansive; Wolfpack's is sharp and tactical
