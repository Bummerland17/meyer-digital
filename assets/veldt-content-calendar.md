## POSTING TIPS

- **LinkedIn:** Post between 8–10am or 12–2pm your audience's local time (US-centric = EST). Engagement drops evenings.
- **X/Twitter:** Post 9–11am EST for max reach. Threads perform best on weekday mornings.
- **Engage actively:** Reply to every comment, especially in weeks 1–2. Early engagement signals boost distribution.
- **Cross-promote:** Reference your HN and IH posts in LinkedIn/X when they're live. "Longer version on Hacker News: [link]"
- **Screenshots:** Screenshot good replies/comments and repost as social proof in weeks 3–4.
