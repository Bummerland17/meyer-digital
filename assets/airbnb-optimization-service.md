**3. Photo Order Optimization**
- Audit their current photo lineup
- Reorder based on Airbnb's proven sequence: Hero shot → Living space → Kitchen → Bedrooms → Bathrooms → Outdoor/Views → Unique features

**5. Delivery Format**

## How to Find STR Owner Contact Info

Jackson Hole has a concentrated, findable STR market. Here's the workflow:

### Method 1: Airbnb Search → Social Media Cross-Reference
1. Go to Airbnb.com, search **Jackson, WY** with dates 2+ months out
2. Open listings one by one — look at the host profile name
3. Search that name + "Jackson Hole" on Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
4. Many hosts are local residents or small investors — they're findable
5. DM on Instagram works surprisingly well for STR owners ("Hey, I work with STR hosts in Jackson...")

### Method 2: Jackson Hole Short-Term Rental Facebook Groups
- Search Facebook: "Jackson Hole Airbnb hosts," "Jackson WY vacation rental," "Teton County STR"
- Groups: *Jackson Hole Vacation Rentals*, *Wyoming STR Owners*, regional landlord groups
- Lurk for 1–2 days, then post or DM active members

### Method 3: Teton County Assessor + STR Permit List
- Wyoming public records: Teton County has an STR permit registry
- Search: `teton county wyoming short term rental permits`
- Cross-reference property addresses with owner names via county assessor site
- Look up owner name → find email via LinkedIn, their property management website, or Google

### Method 4: AirDNA / AllTheRooms Free Tier
- AirDNA shows top-performing listings by market
- Note listing names/host names of underperforming hosts (they need you most)
- Cross-reference via Airbnb host profile

### Method 5: Direct Airbnb Message
- Create or use an existing Airbnb guest account
- Message hosts directly through the platform
- Keep it short: "Hi [Name], I help Jackson Hole hosts optimize their listings for more bookings. Would you be open to a quick 10-min chat?" 
- Conversion rate is low but zero cost

**Pro tip:** Target listings with fewer than 10 reviews, below 4.7 stars, or hosts who haven't updated their listing in 6+ months. These are warm leads.
