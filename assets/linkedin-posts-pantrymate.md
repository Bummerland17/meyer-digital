## Posting Strategy

**Timing:** Post Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am or 5–7pm in your target timezone (US Eastern for max reach)

**Engagement tricks:**
- End every post with a question or call to action in comments
- Reply to every comment within the first hour (this signals LinkedIn to push it further)
- Tag 2–3 people you know personally who might genuinely share it
- Post from your personal profile, not a company page (personal gets 5-10x more reach)

**Cadence:** 1 post per week, rotate topics. After these 3, follow up with:
- "30 days of building PantryMate — here's what I learned"
- "We hit X signups. Here's what worked."
- User testimonials / stories
