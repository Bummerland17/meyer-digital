## FULL PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

You open the fridge. You stare. You close it. You order takeout.

We've all been there. **PantryMate exists to end that moment.**

Type what's in your pantry or fridge — whatever's actually there — and PantryMate's AI matches you to recipes you can make **right now**. No grocery run. No meal plan. No scrolling 47 recipe blogs. Just: *here's your dinner.*

**How it works:**
- Type your ingredients (takes ~30 seconds)
- AI matches recipes instantly from your exact pantry
- Filter by what you can **Cook Now** or what's just **1 Away** (recipes missing only one ingredient)

**Why it's different:**
Most recipe apps show you what to *want* to cook. PantryMate shows you what you *can* cook — tonight, with what you have. It's a decision engine, not an inspiration board.

**Side effects:**
- Less food waste (use what you bought)
- Lower grocery bills (buy only what's actually missing)
- Budget tracking built in so you can see the savings add up

**Pricing:**
- Free — core pantry matching
- Pro — $9.99/mo
- Pro Plus — $14.99/mo
