We're building out our cash buyer network and wanted to see if you're actively buying right now.

### IF THEY ASK "ARE YOU A REAL PERSON / ARE YOU AN AI?"

### IF THEY WANT TO BE REMOVED (DNC)

> **[END CALL — LOG AS DNC REMOVE]**

---

### BUY BOX QUESTIONS

Work through these conversationally — adapt based on their answers:

**1. Active buyer check**
> "Are you actively buying in the Phoenix area right now, or are you taking a break?"
*(If not active: "Got it — is there a timeline when you'd be buying again? Happy to circle back then.")*

**2. Target areas**
> "What areas or zip codes are you most focused on? Are you all over the metro or do you have preferred neighborhoods?"

**3. Property types**
> "Do you focus on single family, multi-family, or both?"

**4. Price range**
> "What price range are you typically buying in? Like, what's your sweet spot?"

**5. ARV percentage**
> "What's your typical max purchase price as a percentage of ARV? Like 65%, 70%...?"
*(If they don't know: "No worries — if I said a property had an ARV of $300k, roughly what would you pay for it all-in?")*

**6. Deal structure preference**
> "Do you prefer getting deals as an assignment of contract, or do you prefer a double close?"
*(Brief explanation if needed: "Assignment means you step into our purchase contract. Double close means two back-to-back closings on the same day.")*

**7. Deal delivery preference**
> "Best way to send you deals — email or text?"

**8. Email for deal flow**
> "What email should I use for sending deal packages?"

---

### CLOSING THE CALL

> "Perfect — I've got your criteria on file. Wolfgang typically puts 2-4 deals per month under contract in Phoenix. When something matches what you're looking for, you'll get a deal package with the address, asking price, ARV estimate, repair estimate, and photos.
>
> First buyer with proof of funds gets it — we don't hold deals. Sound good?"

---

### OBJECTION HANDLING

**"How do you find your deals?"**
> "Direct seller outreach — motivated sellers, off-market situations. We're not on the MLS. Wolfgang contacts sellers directly and gets them under contract."

**"What kind of deals do you typically have?"**
> "Mostly single-family in Phoenix metro, some in the East Valley. Distressed properties, motivated sellers — the kind of deals that need a cash buyer who can move fast. Usually priced at 65-75% of ARV before repairs."

**"I already have wholesalers sending me deals"**
> "Totally fine — more deal flow is usually better, right? If something matches your criteria and the numbers make sense, you'd hear from us. No obligation. Want me to add you to the list anyway?"

**"What's your volume / track record?"**
> "Wolfgang is actively buying and assigning deals in Phoenix. I can have him reach out directly if you'd like to talk shop — he's the real person behind this."
