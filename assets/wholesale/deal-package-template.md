## NOTES FOR FILLING THIS OUT:

**ARV Sources:** Use Zillow Zestimate as a starting point, then verify with Redfin comps. Ask your AI for a quick CMA.

**Repair Estimate:** Rule of thumb —
- Light cosmetic: $10–25k
- Medium rehab: $25–50k
- Full gut: $50–100k+
- Get a contractor walkthrough for anything over $30k

**Assignment Fee:** Typically $5–15k. Set your offer to seller low enough to include this.

**Proof of Funds:** Bank statement showing available funds, or a letter from a hard money lender. You verify before sending contract.

**Closing Timeline:** 14 days is standard. If your title company needs more time, say "flexible for serious buyers."
