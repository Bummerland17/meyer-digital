> **LEGAL NOTE:** In Arizona, a wholesaler may legally assign a purchase contract without a real estate license, provided they are selling their *equitable interest in the contract* — not acting as an agent or broker for the seller. The assignment fee is earned compensation for finding and contracting the deal, not a real estate commission. Consult a licensed Arizona real estate attorney (Arizona State Bar: www.azbar.org) to review this agreement before use. A.R.S. § 32-2101 applies.
