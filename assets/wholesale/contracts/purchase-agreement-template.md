## CONDITION OF PROPERTY (AS-IS)

Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase the property in its **AS-IS, WHERE-IS** condition. Seller makes no warranties or representations regarding the condition of the property, its systems, or its fitness for any purpose.

Buyer acknowledges they are purchasing the property as-is and has had (or waived) the opportunity to inspect. Seller is not required to make any repairs.

---

## ASSIGNMENT CLAUSE

**Buyer expressly reserves the right to assign this contract** to any third party at Buyer's sole discretion, without Seller's consent. An assignment does not release Buyer from obligations under this agreement unless agreed in writing. Buyer may add co-buyers or substitute buyers via assignment at any time prior to closing.

*This clause is the legal basis for wholesale assignment. Buyer is selling their equitable interest in this contract — not the property itself.*

---

## CONTINGENCIES

This agreement is contingent upon:
1. Buyer's satisfactory inspection of the property during the Inspection Period
2. Buyer's (or Assignee's) investor approval and final due diligence
3. Clear and marketable title as confirmed by the title company

If any contingency is not satisfied, Buyer may cancel this agreement and receive a full refund of the EMD within 3 business days.
