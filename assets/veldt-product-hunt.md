## MAKER COMMENT (Personal Founder Note)

**By Wolfgang Meyer, Founder:**

---

Hey Product Hunt 👋

I'm Wolfgang. I'm a solo founder based in Namibia running businesses in Wyoming. I use AI constantly — and I got tired of every AI assistant doing the same thing: answering questions while I still do all the actual work.

I built Veldt because I needed it.

The core frustration: AI is incredibly capable right now, but every product built on top of it is a chatbot. Ask → Answer → You execute. That's not an assistant. That's a very expensive search engine.

Veldt is different in three ways:

**Memory that actually works.** Not a list of saved facts — a persistent model of who you are, who you work with, and what's in progress. Used intelligently, every session.

**Real integrations that write.** Email, calendar, CRM. Veldt doesn't just read your data — it acts on it. It sends the email. It schedules the meeting. It completes the task.

**Proactive execution.** Give it standing instructions and it runs them. "Follow up in 3 days." "Alert me when X changes." You don't re-prompt. It just does it.

I'm building this in public — sharing architecture decisions, progress, and lessons along the way. If you've felt this frustration, I'd love your feedback.

Ask me anything in the comments 🌿

— Wolfgang

P.S. The name comes from the Namibian grassland. It felt right — something earthy, open, and built on real ground.
