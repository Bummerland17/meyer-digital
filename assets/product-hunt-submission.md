## Description

Type what's in your fridge. Get dinner ideas instantly.

No subscriptions required to try — just open pantrymate.net and go.

PantryMate solves the most annoying daily decision most people face: **what's for dinner?** You open the fridge, see a hodgepodge of ingredients, and your brain blanks. PantryMate takes that list and turns it into actual meal ideas in seconds — no recipe scrolling, no "what can I substitute for X," no decision fatigue.

**Why it's different:**
- Works from *your* ingredients, not theoretical pantry setups
- No account required to start — instant value, zero friction
- Built for real kitchens with half-empty bottles and random leftovers
