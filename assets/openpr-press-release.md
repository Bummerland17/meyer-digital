**HEADLINE:**
PantryMate's AI Solves the "$134/Month Fridge Problem" — Dinner Decided in 30 Seconds

**CATEGORY:** Internet & Technology / Food, Beverages & Tobacco

**DATE:** March 2026

---

**BODY:**

PantryMate (pantrymate.net) has launched an AI-powered meal decision engine that addresses one of the most universally frustrating household problems: standing in front of a full fridge with no idea what to cook.

The average American household spends $1,500/year on groceries that end up in the trash and an additional $720/year on takeout ordered on nights when the fridge was full — a combined $134/month "fridge problem" that PantryMate directly targets.

The solution is deliberately simple: users type what's already in their pantry and fridge. PantryMate's AI responds in under 30 seconds with personalized dinner options matched to their ingredients, dietary needs, and cooking skill level.

"Every recipe app assumes you want to search," said Wolfgang Meyer, founder of PantryMate. "We built the opposite — an app that tells you what to cook before you've had a chance to give up and order pizza."

PantryMate is live at pantrymate.net with a free tier offering 3 daily pantry scans. Pro plans start at $9.99/month for unlimited scans, dietary filters, and shopping list generation. A lifetime access option is available for a one-time payment of $49.
