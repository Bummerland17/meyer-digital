## UK-Specific Talking Points

- **CQC compliance:** SmartBook never provides clinical advice, always directs to the treating practitioner for clinical questions. Aligns with CQC requirements for regulated activities.
- **NHS vs. private:** Script handles "do you accept NHS patients?" correctly — states clearly whether the clinic is private-only
- **ICO / GDPR:** Booking data is processed in compliance with UK GDPR; no patient data shared with third parties
- **Bank holidays:** SmartBook works 365 days — significant for aesthetics clinics that lose bookings on Bank Holidays
- **Tone:** UK patients expect professional but approachable. Avoid overly salesy US-style scripts.

## Australia-Specific Talking Points

- **After-hours calls:** Australians increasingly call outside business hours; answering 7–9pm captures working parents and professionals
- **Health fund FAQs:** SmartBook can be trained to answer basic health fund questions ("Do you accept HBF?") without booking staff involvement
- **AHPRA awareness:** Never claims to provide dental advice; always escalates clinical questions to registered practitioners
- **Emergency triage:** For dental emergencies, SmartBook identifies urgency and routes to emergency line or provides nearest after-hours option
- **Geography:** Differentiate by suburb — "practices in Paddington," "Toowong area" etc. — Australians are very suburb-aware
