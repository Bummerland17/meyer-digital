## HOW TO USE THIS
1. Click the Facebook or Instagram link
2. Hit "Message" on their page
3. Copy-paste the DM below
4. Swap in their name if you know it
5. Done — takes 2 min per business

Response rate: ~10-20% on Instagram DMs, ~5-15% on Facebook for local businesses.
Best time to send: weekday 9-11am or 7-9pm their local time.
