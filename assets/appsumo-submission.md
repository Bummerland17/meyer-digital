## Deal Overview (~300 words)

Every night, millions of people open the fridge, stare blankly, and end up ordering takeout — not because they're out of food, but because they can't figure out what to make with what they have. It's the "what's for dinner?" problem, and it quietly costs households hundreds of dollars a month in wasted groceries and impulsive delivery orders.

**PantryMate solves this with AI.**

Snap a photo of your fridge, pantry, or grocery receipt and PantryMate's AI instantly reads your ingredients and generates real, cookable meal suggestions in under 30 seconds. No manual ingredient entry. No scrolling through recipe blogs. No "oh I don't have that one thing" frustration. Just: here's what you have, here's what you can make tonight.

Behind the scenes, PantryMate uses computer vision to recognize ingredients from photos and AI to match them against your dietary preferences, skill level, and time constraints. It learns what you like over time and gets sharper with every scan. It also tracks your pantry state between shopping trips so you always know what's running low.

**Who it's for:**

- Busy families who want home-cooked meals without the mental overhead
- Budget-conscious cooks trying to stretch grocery dollars
- Meal preppers who want smarter Sunday planning
- Anyone who's ever thrown out a fridge full of "I forgot about that" food

PantryMate isn't a recipe app — it's a decision engine. The difference matters. Recipe apps show you what you *could* make. PantryMate tells you what you *should* make with what you actually have, right now.

The average American household wastes $1,500/year in food. PantryMate pays for itself in saved groceries within the first month — and that's before you count the time saved and the takeout orders you skip.

## Key Features

- 📷 **Instant Ingredient Scanning** — Photograph your fridge, pantry shelf, or grocery receipt; AI reads everything automatically (no manual entry)
- 🍽️ **AI Meal Suggestions in 30 Seconds** — Get 3–5 cookable dinner ideas matched to your exact inventory, preferences, and time available
- 🧠 **Learns Your Preferences** — Dietary filters (vegan, gluten-free, low-carb, allergies), cuisine preferences, and cooking skill level — all personalized
- 📦 **Pantry State Tracking** — Maintain a running inventory between shop trips; get low-stock alerts before you run out
- 🗓️ **Meal Planning & Shopping Lists** — Plan the week ahead and auto-generate grocery lists for missing ingredients
- ♻️ **Use-It-Up Mode** — Specifically surfaces meals that use ingredients closest to their expiry date, cutting waste before it happens
- 📱 **Mobile-First, Instant Access** — Works from any browser on mobile or desktop; no app download required
