Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2025

PantryMate ("we", "us", or "our") operates the PantryMate mobile application and website at pantrymate.net (collectively, the "Service"). This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We do not store plaintext passwords.

Pantry and meal data: Ingredients you enter and meal suggestions you save are stored to provide the core Service. This data is tied to your account.

Usage data: We collect basic analytics such as feature usage frequency and session length to improve the product. This data is not tied to personally identifiable information.

Device information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the PantryMate Service
  • To generate meal suggestions based on your pantry contents
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • To communicate product updates (you can opt out at any time)
  • To diagnose and fix bugs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

We use the following third-party services to operate PantryMate:

  • Supabase – database and authentication hosting
  • Stripe – payment processing for Pro subscriptions (we never see your card details)
  • OpenAI / AI providers – meal suggestion generation (ingredient lists may be processed; no personal identifiers are shared)

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days. Anonymized usage analytics may be retained longer.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your pantry and meal data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@pantrymate.net.

6. Cookies

The PantryMate website uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

PantryMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for all data in transit and follow security best practices for data at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@pantrymate.net.
