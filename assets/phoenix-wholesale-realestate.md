## MAO Formula (Maximum Allowable Offer)
```
ARV (After Repair Value)
× 70% (iBuyer/fund rule)
− Repair Estimate
= MAO (your max offer to seller)

Your Assignment Fee = What buyer pays you − MAO
```

**Worked Example — West Phoenix SFR:**
- ARV: $285,000
- 70% = $199,500
- Repairs: $25,000
- MAO to seller: $174,500
- You lock it up at $174,500
- Sell contract to cash buyer at $184,500
- **Your fee: $10,000**
