## 💸 Best $10–20 Ad Spend Recommendation

### Winner: Pinterest Ads — $15 budget

**Why Pinterest wins at this budget:**
- Food is Pinterest's #1 category. Meal planning content is searched daily by 100M+ users.
- Minimum campaign: $2/day (you can run $15 over a week)
- CPCs for food/recipe content: $0.10–$0.50 (vs. Google at $1–4)
- Unlike Google/Facebook, Pinterest intent is high — people are *looking* for recipes, not just scrolling
- The audience (women 25–45, food-interested, home cooks) is *exactly* PantryMate's customer

**Campaign setup:**
- Platform: ads.pinterest.com
- Ad format: Static pin or video pin
- Image: Clean shot of a meal being made, or phone showing the app output
- Headline: "What's in your fridge? Get dinner ideas in 30 seconds."
- CTA: "Try Free"
- Target URL: pantrymate.net
- Targeting: Interests → Food & Drink → Cooking, Meal Planning, Budget Meals
- Keywords: "meal planning", "what to cook tonight", "pantry recipes", "easy dinners"
- Budget: $2/day × 7 days = $14

**Expected results:**
- Impressions: 5,000–20,000
- Clicks: 50–150 at $0.10–0.30 CPC
- Signups (freemium, ~15% conversion): 7–22 free users
- Paid conversions (~3–5% of free users over 30 days): 0–1 paying users immediately, but pipeline is building

**Pinterest Ads note:** Account creation is instant at ads.pinterest.com. No credit required upfront for some campaign types.

---

### Alternative: Facebook/Instagram — $15 budget

**If Pinterest doesn't work:**
- Minimum meaningful FB/IG spend: $5/day
- Run for 3 days = $15
- Target: Women 25–45, interests: meal planning, cooking, food waste, Tasty, AllRecipes
- Instagram Stories or Feed ad (single image)
- Headline: "Staring at your fridge? Let AI figure out dinner."
- Expected CPC: $0.50–$1.50
- Expected clicks: 10–30 on $15 budget
- Expected signups: 2–5 free users

**Verdict:** Pinterest wins for this budget because CPCs are lower and food intent is higher. Facebook's minimum to get meaningful data is closer to $30–50.

## 🎯 Product Hunt Launch Guide

Product Hunt can drive 200–2,000 signups in a single day if done right.

**When to launch:** Tuesday–Thursday, 12:01am PST (so your launch gets a full day)

**Pre-launch prep (1 week before):**
- Create a PH account and engage with other products (upvote, comment) to build karma
- Find a "hunter" — someone with 500+ followers on PH who will submit/feature your product. DM hunters on Twitter: "I'm launching an AI meal planning app, would you be interested in hunting it?"
- Prepare: logo, tagline, 3–5 screenshots, a demo GIF or video (60–90 seconds)
- Prepare a "PH community" comment ready to post the second you launch: "Hey PH! I'm Wolfgang, solo founder…"

**Best tagline for PH:**
> "Type what's in your fridge → get dinner ideas in 30 seconds"

**During launch:**
- Post in every relevant FB group, Discord, Slack: "We're live on Product Hunt today!"
- Send to email list
- Post on LinkedIn
- Ask friends to upvote (but NOT vote brigading — PH will penalize)

**Expected result:** 50–500 upvotes → 200–2,000 site visitors → 20–200 signups
