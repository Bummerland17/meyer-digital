## 🤖 SECTION 5: AI COURSE CREATION PIPELINE

### The Full Stack (Cost-Efficient Build)

**Phase 1: Ideation & Outline (Day 1) — FREE**
- ChatGPT/Claude: Generate course outline, module structure, learning outcomes
- Prompt: "Create a 5-module course outline for [topic]. Include 4-6 lessons per module with clear outcomes. Target audience: [who]. End result: [transformation]."
- Validate demand: Check Udemy for competing courses with 1K+ reviews

**Phase 2: Script Writing (Days 2–3) — FREE**
- Claude/ChatGPT: Write lesson scripts (750–1,000 words per lesson = ~7 min video)
- Use Canva for slide templates (free or $15/mo)
- Structure: Hook → Teach → Apply → Summary every lesson

**Phase 3: Video Production (Days 4–8)**

Option A — **No Face (Fastest)**:
- **ElevenLabs** ($22/mo) — Clone your own voice or use a pre-built voice for narration
- **Loom** (free tier) — Screen record slides/software demos
- **Descript** ($24/mo) — Edit video by editing transcript; AI removes filler words
- **Canva** — Slide design and animated visuals
- Total: ~$46/mo in tools, course built in 4–5 days

Option B — **AI Avatar (No camera required)**:
- **HeyGen** ($29/mo Starter) — Generate talking-head videos from scripts
- **ElevenLabs** — Voice narration layer
- Professional quality without filming
- Total: ~$51/mo, slightly longer build (6–8 days)

Option C — **Face Cam (Most trust, most time)**:
- Smartphone camera or webcam
- Teleprompter app (free) for scripts
- **Descript** for editing
- Total: ~$24/mo tools + filming time

**Phase 4: Platform & Launch (Days 9–10)**
- **Udemy** — Upload and submit for review (3–5 days approval); built-in SEO and audience
- **Gumroad** — Instant publish; no approval; 10% fee; need own audience
- **Lemon Squeezy** — Great for higher-priced courses; handles global tax (VAT)

7. **Kajabi data confirms:** 75% of six-figure creators run **5+ revenue streams**. Don't put all eggs in Udemy — build the owned asset (email list) from day one.
