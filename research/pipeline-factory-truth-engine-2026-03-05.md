### n8n Template Marketplace — How It Works

**Official n8n.io marketplace:**
- Free to submit templates
- Templates are public and free (n8n uses them for discoverability/SEO)
- Creators "earn via affiliates program" — not direct sales of templates
- n8n has 8,500+ free community templates — too many to stand out on n8n.io alone
- **Strategy:** Post free "teaser" templates on n8n.io for discovery → link to paid full versions on own site

**n8nmarket.com (third-party):**
- Explicitly designed for buying/selling n8n, Make, Zapier templates
- Has category browsing, seller profiles
- Also supports "custom automation projects" — clients can request custom builds
- Seller pricing and revenue share not publicly stated, but described as "premium rates"

**haveworkflow.com:**
- Marketplace specifically for Zapier, Make, n8n workflows
- "Turn your automation skills into a revenue stream. Sell your pre-built workflows on our marketplace."
- Launched to capture the gap between free n8n community and expensive custom work

**Key insight:** These marketplaces are young and have low competition. A seller with 10–20 high-quality, well-documented pipelines could become a top seller quickly.
