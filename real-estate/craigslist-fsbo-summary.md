## ⚠️ Important Notes for Wholesale Outreach

### Why Most Leads Don't Have Embedded Phone Numbers
Craigslist uses an **anonymous email relay system** — sellers don't need to post their phone because buyers 
contact them via the CL reply system. Only ~3 of 60 sellers included their phone directly in the listing text.

### Contact Strategy for Craigslist Relay Leads
Each lead has an `email` field with the CL reply URL. Here's how to use it:

1. **Visit the listing URL** directly
2. Click **"Reply"** → Craigslist sends your message to the seller's email
3. **Seller replies** to your inquiry → you now have their direct email
4. **Follow up** to get their phone number

**Recommended message**:
> "Hi! I saw your listing on Craigslist. I'm a cash buyer and very interested in your property. 
> Can you share your contact number so we can discuss? - [Your name]"
