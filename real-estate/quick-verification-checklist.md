## 🏠 BEFORE PURSUING ANY SELLER DEAL

| # | Check | Free Resource | Pass? |
|---|-------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | Owner of record matches seller's name | County assessor website | ☐ |
| 2 | Deed history looks normal (no recent rapid flips) | County recorder | ☐ |
| 3 | No undisclosed liens or judgments | County recorder + IRS EFTS | ☐ |
| 4 | Taxes current | County tax collector | ☐ |
| 5 | Seller showed valid photo ID (or video ID for remote) | In-person / video call | ☐ |
| 6 | All owners/co-owners identified and available to sign | Deed search | ☐ |
| 7 | Occupancy status confirmed (vacant / tenant / owner) | Site visit | ☐ |
| 8 | No other purchase agreements in place | Seller attestation + PSA warranty | ☐ |
| 9 | Estate/LLC sellers have verified legal authority | Letters testamentary / SOS lookup | ☐ |
| 10 | Drove by the property before scheduling appointment | Google Maps + physical drive-by | ☐ |

**→ ALL 10 must be ✅ before signing a contract.**

---

## 💰 BEFORE SHARING ANY DEAL WITH A BUYER

| # | Check | Free Resource | Pass? |
|---|-------|--------------|-------|
| 1 | Buyer named 3+ verifiable closed deals | County recorder deed search | ☐ |
| 2 | POF is current (< 30 days), shows transaction history | Review + bank verification call | ☐ |
| 3 | POF account name matches buyer's legal name/entity | Side-by-side comparison | ☐ |
| 4 | Buyer's entity verified at Secretary of State | State SOS website | ☐ |
| 5 | 2 references checked by phone | Personal calls | ☐ |
| 6 | Buyer agreement / NDA signed before sharing address | Your agreement | ☐ |
| 7 | EMD goes to title company escrow (confirmed in writing) | Title company confirmation | ☐ |
| 8 | Assignment agreement signed with non-circumvention clause | Your assignment agreement | ☐ |
| 9 | Title company has copy of assignment + fee instruction | Email to title company | ☐ |
| 10 | No Google/BBB complaints found for buyer/entity | Google search + BBB lookup | ☐ |

**→ ALL 10 must be ✅ before sharing deal details.**

---

## 🚩 INSTANT WALK-AWAY RED FLAGS

Stop. Do not proceed. Walk away if you hear:

- ❌ "Send the deposit directly to me"
- ❌ "I'm overseas but I own the property"
- ❌ "We need to close in 3 days"
- ❌ "You don't need to verify all that"
- ❌ "My friend/brother is handling the sale for the owner"
- ❌ "Let's skip the title company"
- ❌ "Wire instructions changed — use this new account"
- ❌ POF with no transaction history — just a balance
- ❌ Seller doesn't know basic facts about their property
- ❌ Multiple red flags even if each seems minor

## 📋 EARNEST MONEY — IRON RULE

```
ALL EMD → Title Company Escrow ONLY
Never to: you, buyer, seller, anyone's personal account

Wire instructions change? → STOP → Call title company
at their official number (from their website, NOT the email)
```

---

## ✅ CONTRACT MUST-HAVES

☐ Title contingency clause  
☐ Ownership warranty by seller  
☐ Occupancy disclosure  
☐ "And/or assigns" in buyer name  
☐ Inspection/due diligence period  
☐ EMD to named title company  
☐ All known encumbrances disclosed  

**Assignment Agreement adds:**  
☐ Non-circumvention clause  
☐ Assignment fee amount + title company disbursement instruction  
☐ No re-assignment without consent
