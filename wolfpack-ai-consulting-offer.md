1. **Audit** is the low-risk entry point. Client pays $2,500, gets a roadmap. You get paid to do discovery. Natural upsell → Product 2.
2. **Custom Agent Build** is the main recurring revenue engine. Setup fee + $1,497/mo retainer compounds quickly. 5 clients = $7,485/mo MRR.

- **Marketing agencies (10-50 staff):** Pain = manual reporting, client onboarding, proposal writing, lead follow-up

- **Medical practices:** Pain = appointment reminders, patient intake forms, billing queries
- **SMB owners (any vertical):** Pain = the owner is doing everything — AI buys back their time
