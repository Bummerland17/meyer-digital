# AI Sales Agent Script Bundle
**5 Complete Outbound Call Scripts + Vapi & ElevenLabs Deployment Guide**

## What's Inside

This bundle contains five production-ready AI sales agent scripts built for real outbound calling campaigns. Each script has been structured for use with Vapi (the AI phone calling platform) and can be deployed with an ElevenLabs voice in under an hour.

These aren't generic "feel free to customize" scripts. They're structured with real objection handling, branching logic, and closing sequences that work for cold outreach.

## SCRIPT 1: SmartBook AI — Dental Practice Appointment Booking

**Target:** Dental office managers and practice owners  
**Goal:** Book a demo call for SmartBook AI automated scheduling  
**Tone:** Professional, efficient, no fluff

---

### Opening

> "Hi, this is Alex calling for [Practice Name]. I'm reaching out because we work with dental practices in [City] that are tired of playing phone tag with patients trying to book appointments. Is [Contact Name] available for 90 seconds?"

**If gatekeeper:**
> "Totally understand. Could you let them know Alex from SmartBook called? We've got a quick case study from a practice in [nearby city] that cut no-shows by 40% — I'll call back at [time]. What's a better window?"

---

### Opening (Direct Contact)

> "Hi [Name], this is Alex. Quick question — how are you currently handling appointment reminders and last-minute cancellations?"

**[PAUSE — let them answer]**

---

### Pivot Based on Response

**If: "We call patients manually"**
> "That's what most practices are doing. How much time per week is your front desk spending on that?"

**If: "We use [competitor]"**
> "Got it. Are you happy with the no-show rate, or is that still a problem?"

**If: "We have a system"**
> "Perfect — are you open to seeing how other practices are handling it differently? Takes about 12 minutes."

---

### The Pitch (30 seconds)

> "We built SmartBook AI specifically for dental offices. It handles appointment reminders, confirmations, and rebooking through automated voice calls — sounds like a real person, works 24/7, and plugs into your existing scheduling software. Most practices cut no-shows by 30-50% in the first 60 days. Would a 12-minute demo this week make sense?"

---

### Objection Handling

**"We're too busy right now"**
> "That's exactly when it helps most. When are things typically less hectic — end of week? I can do 7 minutes on Thursday at 3pm if that works."

**"How much does it cost?"**
> "Depends on appointment volume, but most practices see it pay for itself in the first week from recovered no-show appointments. I can show you the math on the demo. What's your current no-show rate?"

**"We're not interested"**
> "Completely fair. One last question — is it more that the timing's off, or you genuinely don't have a no-show problem? Most practices I talk to have both."

---

### Close

> "Great — I'll send a calendar invite right now. What email should I use?"

## SCRIPT 2: Local SEO Agency — Small Business Owners

**Target:** Restaurant owners, service businesses, local shops  
**Goal:** Book a free SEO audit call  
**Tone:** Direct, friendly, consultative

---

### Opening

> "Hi [Name], this is Marcus. I was just looking at your Google listing for [Business Name] — do you have 60 seconds? I noticed something that's probably costing you customers."

---

### The Hook

> "When someone searches '[service] in [city]' on Google, your listing is showing up on page [X]. The three businesses above you are getting roughly 70% of the clicks. I can walk you through exactly what they're doing that you aren't — takes about 10 minutes."

---

### Objection Handling

**"We already have an SEO company"**
> "Are they reporting to you monthly? What's your current ranking for your main keyword?"
[If they don't know:]
> "That's actually the most common issue — agencies that don't report clearly. I'll just show you where you stand right now, no strings attached."

**"I don't have a budget for this"**
> "The audit is free. If what I show you makes sense, we talk. If not, you walk away knowing exactly where you stand on Google."

**"We get most of our customers by word of mouth"**
> "That's great. The businesses I work with said the same thing right before they started getting 20-30 new customers a month from search. Word of mouth caps out. Google doesn't."

---

### Close

> "I'll pull up your current stats while we're on the phone — takes 2 minutes. Then if it's worth a deeper look, we book 20 minutes. Fair?"

## SCRIPT 3: App Development Outreach — Startup Founders & Operators

**Target:** Non-technical founders with an app idea, operators who mentioned needing software  
**Goal:** Qualify and book a scoping call  
**Tone:** Builder-to-builder, peer energy

---

### Opening

> "Hey [Name], this is Jordan. Saw you mentioned on [LinkedIn/Twitter/event] that you're working on [app/tool idea]. I build apps for founders — specifically the 'I have an idea but no dev team' stage. Do you have a quick minute?"

---

### Discovery Questions

> "What are you trying to build, and what's the blocker right now — is it budget, finding the right developer, or just not knowing where to start?"

**[Let them talk. This is where 80% of the sale happens.]**

---

### Pivot

> "Okay, here's what I'm hearing: [summarize their problem]. We've built [relevant example] for a similar situation — went from idea to working MVP in [X weeks]. Are you trying to validate first or build something more polished?"

---

### Objection Handling

**"It's too expensive"**
> "What's your current budget range? We've done MVPs for $3k and full products for $30k. The first step is usually a scoping call where I can give you an honest number."

**"I'm not ready yet"**
> "What does 'ready' look like for you? Sometimes people spend 6 months getting ready when they could have a working prototype in 4 weeks."

**"I already have a developer"**
> "How's that going? A lot of people I talk to have a developer but no traction. I can at least give you a second opinion on your current approach."

---

### Close

> "Let me do a 20-minute scoping call — no pitch, just map out what you'd need to build. If it's not a fit, I'll tell you. If it is, we'll know exactly what it costs. Does [day] work?"

## SCRIPT 4: Real Estate Wholesale — Motivated Sellers

**Target:** Homeowners facing foreclosure, probate, divorce, or distressed situations  
**Goal:** Schedule an in-person walkthrough  
**Tone:** Empathetic, no pressure, genuine

---

### Opening

> "Hi, I'm looking for [Name]. This is Chris — I'm a local real estate investor in [area]. I send letters to homeowners who might be considering selling their home without listing it. Did you get a letter from us recently, or is this the first time we're connecting?"

---

### Discovery

> "I don't want to waste your time — can I ask, are you at a stage where selling is actually on the table, or are you just exploring options?"

**[PAUSE]**

> "Okay, good. What's driving the consideration — is it mostly the property itself, a life situation, or just timing?"

---

### The Value Prop

> "What we do is different from listing with an agent. There's no repairs, no showings, no 3-month wait. We make a cash offer, you pick the closing date — we've done 30 days and we've done 6 months. Whatever works for your situation. The offer might be lower than market, but most sellers we work with are trading the convenience for certainty."

---

### Objection Handling

**"I want full market value"**
> "That's completely fair, and listing on the MLS might get you there — depends on the condition of the property and your timeline. Can I ask what the property needs in terms of repairs?"

**"I need to think about it"**
> "Absolutely. What would help you think through it — is it seeing an actual number, or are there other options you're weighing?"

**"I'm not sure about the price"**
> "I can't give you a number until I see the property. Takes 20 minutes. If the number doesn't work for you, no hard feelings — at least you know what a cash offer looks like."

---

### Close

> "Could I come by [day] just to see the property? 20 minutes, no commitment. I'll give you a number on the spot."

## SCRIPT 5: Real Estate — Buyer Lead Follow-Up

**Target:** Leads from Zillow, Realtor.com, or open houses  
**Goal:** Re-engage, qualify, and schedule a buyer consultation  
**Tone:** Helpful, not salesy

---

### Opening

> "Hey [Name], this is Sam from [Agency]. You inquired about a property at [address] a little while back — just wanted to circle back and see if you're still looking, or if you've found what you need."

---

### Discovery

> "Where are you in the process — are you actively looking right now, or was it more of an exploratory browse?"

**If actively looking:**
> "What's changed since you first looked? Have your must-haves shifted at all?"

**If exploratory:**
> "What would need to change for it to feel like the right time — is it interest rates, saving more, or just life stuff?"

---

### Value Add

> "I can set you up with a search that alerts you the second something hits the market matching what you want — before it shows up on Zillow. Most of my buyers find their home that way now. Would that be useful even if you're not in a rush?"

---

### Objection Handling

**"The market is too hot/expensive"**
> "It is. That's why timing matters more than ever — the buyers who win right now are the ones who are ready to move fast. I can walk you through what that looks like in a 20-minute call."

**"We're working with another agent"**
> "No problem at all. If that's working out well, stick with it. If you ever want a second opinion on a specific property or area, I'm happy to help."

---

### Close

> "Let me send you a few properties that match what you described — just to keep you in the loop. What email should I use?"

# DEPLOYMENT GUIDE: Vapi + ElevenLabs Setup

## Step 1: Create Your Vapi Account

1. Go to vapi.ai and create an account
2. Navigate to **Phone Numbers** → Add a number (US local number is ~$2/mo)
3. Navigate to **Assistants** → Create New Assistant

## Step 2: Configure the Assistant

In the Assistant settings:

- **First Message:** Paste your script's Opening section
- **System Prompt:** Add context about who the agent is (company name, product, goal)
- **Voice Provider:** Select ElevenLabs (see Step 3)
- **End Call Function:** Set trigger phrases ("not interested," "take me off your list," "goodbye")

### Sample System Prompt (SmartBook AI)

```
You are Alex, an outbound sales representative for SmartBook AI. You're calling dental practices to introduce our AI-powered appointment booking system. 

Your goal is to book a 12-minute demo call.

Key rules:
- Be friendly and direct, not robotic
- Ask questions before pitching
- If they're not interested, thank them and end the call politely
- Never push more than twice after a "no"
- Record: interested/not interested, best callback time, and their current no-show challenge
```

## Step 3: ElevenLabs Voice Setup

1. Go to elevenlabs.io → Create account
2. Navigate to **Voice Lab** → Add Voice → Instant Voice Clone (or use a pre-built voice)
3. Recommended voices for sales:
   - **Rachel** — warm, confident (good for dental/service)
   - **Adam** — professional, clear (good for B2B)
   - **Elli** — friendly, approachable (good for local business)
4. Copy your Voice ID from ElevenLabs
5. In Vapi Assistant settings → Voice → ElevenLabs → paste Voice ID
6. Set stability: 0.6, similarity: 0.75 (natural without being unpredictable)

## Step 4: Connect to a CRM (Optional but Recommended)

Use Vapi's webhook to push call outcomes to:
- **Airtable** (free, easy)
- **HubSpot** (free tier works)
- **Notion database**

Vapi sends a JSON payload after each call with transcript, duration, and outcome.

## Step 5: Test Before Going Live

- Use Vapi's built-in test call feature
- Call yourself 5 times with different objections
- Adjust the system prompt based on where it breaks
- Add any missing objections to the script

## Step 6: Launch

- Start with 20-50 calls/day to test
- Review transcripts weekly
- Iterate on the opening — that's where 80% of calls are won or lost
