Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 4, 2026  ·  App package: com.pantrymate.app
Short version: We collect only what we need to run PantryMate. We don't sell your data. We don't rent it. We don't do anything creepy with it. Full details below.

1. Who We Are

PantryMate is a meal planning and pantry management app developed and operated by PantryMate ("we", "our", "us"). You can reach us at hello@pantrymate.net.

Our app is available on Android (Google Play) at the package name com.pantrymate.app. This policy applies to all versions of PantryMate.

2. What Data We Collect

Data typeWhat exactlyWhy we need it
Account Email address, hashed password Account creation, login, password reset
Pantry data Ingredient names, quantities, expiry dates you enter Generating recipe suggestions, expiry reminders
Meal plans Recipes you save and schedule Displaying your meal calendar and shopping lists
Usage data Feature interactions, session length (anonymized) Understanding what works, fixing what doesn't
Payment data Subscription status (via Google Play or Stripe) Gating Pro Plus features. We never see raw card numbers.
Device info OS version, device model, app version Debugging crashes and compatibility issues

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, or any other device permission unless you explicitly grant it for a specific feature (e.g., camera for barcode scanning). Permissions are always optional.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To run the app and provide the features you signed up for
  • To send you transactional emails (password reset, receipt) — never marketing spam unless you opt in
  • To show you expiry reminders for pantry items you've added
  • To diagnose bugs and improve performance
  • To process your Pro Plus subscription and verify access

We do not use your data to train AI models, build advertising profiles, or share it with data brokers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to power PantryMate:

ServicePurposeTheir privacy policy
Supabase Database & authentication (stores your account and pantry data) supabase.com/privacy
Stripe Payment processing for Pro Plus subscriptions (web/direct billing) stripe.com/privacy
Google Play App distribution and in-app billing policies.google.com/privacy

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We choose partners who take data seriously. Your pantry data is stored in Supabase — it is not shared with Stripe or Google beyond what's necessary to process payments and distribute the app.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your data within 30 days from our systems and request deletion from Supabase. Anonymized usage statistics may be retained longer.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct — fix inaccurate data (most can be done in-app)
  • Delete — request full account and data deletion
  • Export — receive your pantry and meal plan data in a portable format
  • Opt out — unsubscribe from any marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@pantrymate.net with the subject "Privacy Request". We'll respond within 14 days.

If you're in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Children's Privacy

PantryMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: HTTPS everywhere, hashed passwords (never stored in plain text), and access controls on our database. No system is 100% secure — if you discover a vulnerability, please email us responsibly.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify you via email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of PantryMate after changes means you accept the updated policy.
